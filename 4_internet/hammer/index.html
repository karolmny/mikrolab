Download Erlang Packages

Hey, we are currently working on this page to make all packages easily downloadable to you on desktop; in the meantime you can contact us directly at general@erlang-solutions.com with any questions, or subscribe to our Erlang & Elixir Newsletter.

Installation and dependency resolution is straight-forward with our standard Erlang/OTP and Elixir download packages. In the event of installation issues, we offer support for both Erlang and Elixir and we include the standardised Erlang/OTP testing results.
